Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

  • Account info — the nickname and password you choose. Passwords are handled by Google Firebase Authentication; we never see or store your raw password.
  • Content you create — posts and comments in Sharing Thoughts, and any settings you configure (tracked topics, sectors, companies, news sources, schedule).
  • Usage data — standard technical logs (e.g. request info) generated by running the Service on Render's infrastructure.

2. How we use it

We use this information to run the Service: to sign you in, show your shared posts to other users, remember your preferences, and generate your daily legal briefings and research results.

3. Third parties that process data on our behalf

  • Google Firebase — authentication and data storage.
  • Anthropic — receives the text/images you ask it to analyze (e.g. a story research request) to generate a response.
  • OpenAI — if you use the "Listen" audio feature, your briefing text is sent to OpenAI to generate speech.
  • CourtListener / SEC EDGAR — public legal and financial databases we query on your behalf; no personal data is sent beyond your search terms.
  • News sources you add — we fetch publicly available pages from the URLs you configure.

4. What's public

Your nickname and anything you post in Sharing Thoughts (including comments) are visible to anyone using the Service. Don't share anything there you want to keep private.

5. Data retention & deletion

Your account and content persist until you ask us to delete them. Email lexalmonds@gmail.com to request deletion of your account and associated data.

6. Children's privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Service changes. We'll update the "Last updated" date above when we do.

8. Contact

Questions about your data? Email lexalmonds@gmail.com.
